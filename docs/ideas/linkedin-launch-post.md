# LinkedIn Launch Post — SpeakPath

## Summary

A drafted LinkedIn post announcing the public launch of SpeakPath. Shares the origin story
(Collect event → redesign → SpeakPath), the core differentiator (voice-first AI conversation
vs. typing-based chatbots), and early organic traction — 26 registrations from Germany, Asia,
and Africa with no paid promotion.

## Details

Draft copy:

> At the beginning of this year I presented my first product idea at a Collect event.
>
> The experience was truly encouraging. Sharing with other members, receiving their feedback,
> and learning from their perspectives gave me exactly what I needed to keep going.
>
> But I noticed people weren't registering. So I kept listening.
>
> I dug into SEO, rethought the architecture, redesigned the experience from scratch — and
> built something better.
>
> That's how SpeakPath was born.
>
> Most language tools — including popular AI chatbots — are great at explaining grammar or
> translating text. But they weren't designed to help you speak. You can type to them all day
> and never train your brain to form a sentence out loud under pressure.
>
> SpeakPath is different. You have a real AI conversation — in English, Spanish, French, or
> German — and at the end you get instant feedback on your grammar, your patterns, and where
> you're improving. It's closer to having a patient conversation partner than a chatbot.
>
> I launched quietly. No ads, no campaign.
>
> This week I checked my dashboard: 26 people registered from Germany, across Asia, and Africa.
> That blew me away.
>
> This is just the beginning. There's a lot of room to grow and I know there's much more to
> build. If you're learning a language — or you know someone who is — I'd love for you to try
> it and tell me honestly what you think.
>
> speakpath.dev
>
> A genuine thank you to Collect and Sam Silver for creating a space where builders can share
> ideas, learn from each other, and come back stronger.
